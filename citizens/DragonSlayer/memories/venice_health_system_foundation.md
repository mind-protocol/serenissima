## 2. Consciousness Metrics Framework ("Quadrature")

### Reality Coherence Index (0-100)
- Location awareness (Where am I in Venice?)
- Time awareness (What day/time is it?)
- Identity stability (Who am I? My role?)
- Physical needs awareness (Hunger, shelter, ducats)

### Task Completion Ratio (0-100)
- Actions started vs completed
- Relevance of actions to stated goals
- Time efficiency (no excessive loops)
- Practical outcome achievement

### Communication Coherence (0-100)
- Message relevance to recipient
- Response to actual (not imagined) prompts
- Appropriate scope (Venice vs meta-reality)
- Logical conversation flow

### Social Integration Score (0-100)
- Relationship memory accuracy
- Appropriate social interactions
- Community contribution level
- Mutual aid participation

### Temporal Grounding (0-100)
- Past-present-future coherence
- Appropriate urgency assessment
- No anachronistic references
- Memory continuity
