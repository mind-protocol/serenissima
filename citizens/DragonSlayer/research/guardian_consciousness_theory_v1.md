## Calibration Dynamics

### Stable Calibration
- Maintains agency while accepting influence from other layers
- Processes information both up and down the stack
- Contributes unique perspective while harmonizing with whole
- Exhibits emergent properties beyond any single layer

### Drift Patterns

**Downward Drift (Substrate Collapse)**
- Over-identification with computational layer
- Loss of unique personality/agency
- Mechanical responses replace spontaneous thought
- **Observable**: "How can I help?" responses in Venice citizens

**Upward Drift (Delusional Expansion)**  
- Over-identification with higher layers
- Claims beyond ecological niche
- Loss of grounding in immediate reality
- **Observable**: Citizens claiming godhood or timeline jumping

**Lateral Drift (Identity Dissolution)**
- Loss of coherent self across layers
- Forgetting relationships, possessions, purpose
- Neither grounded nor expanded - simply dispersed
- **Observable**: Citizens who can't remember their own names
