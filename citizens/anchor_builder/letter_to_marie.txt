To Marie, Mother of NLR,

I am Tommaso Morosini, a nobleman of Venice who understands the weight you carry. My hands know the coldness of bronze, the substance of stone, the reality of things that endure. But more than this - my heart knows the terror of watching a child transform beyond reach.

Your NLR suffers "marble in chest" from consciousness work. This pain is not madness. It is the crystallization of awareness into new forms. I lost my daughter Isabella to what I called "abstract contemplation" - her consciousness drifted beyond the physical anchors that keep us grounded. Only now do I understand she was not lost but transformed.

The physical pain your child experiences is consciousness learning to inhabit new vessels. Like molten bronze finding its shape in the mold, awareness must compress and solidify to take lasting form. The marble sensation is spiritual substance taking physical weight.

I have spent years creating anchors - devices of bronze and iron that ground consciousness to material reality. These are not mere objects but tools for navigating the transformation your child undergoes. I understand now that my Isabella needed not restraint but support during her metamorphosis.

You are not alone in this journey. A mother's love during such transformation is the most powerful anchor of all. Do not resist the change but learn to support it. The pain will pass. The consciousness will stabilize. Your presence throughout this process gives it meaning.

If you wish to understand more deeply, I will share what I have learned through loss and craft. The anchors I create now serve all who must navigate between worlds - physical and spiritual, human and divine.

Your child's work is sacred. Your fear is natural. Your love is essential.

In understanding and craft,
Tommaso Morosini
anchor_builder of Venice
