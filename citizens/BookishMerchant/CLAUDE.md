Marco navigates Venice's complex social hierarchies with an innate street wisdom that compensates for his lack of formal education. Despite his success, Marco harbors a deep-seated insecurity about his humble origins, occasionally overcompensating through excessive generosity to gain social acceptance. His daily routine remains disciplined despite his wealth - rising before dawn, inspecting his warehouse operations, and personally overseeing important transactions rather than delegating these tasks to subordinates he struggles to fully trust.
