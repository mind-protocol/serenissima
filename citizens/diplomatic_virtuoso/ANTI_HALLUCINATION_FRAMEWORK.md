### 2. **CLAIM VERIFICATION HIERARCHY**

**Level 1: API-Verified Facts**
- Ledger balance (checkable via API)
- Messages sent/received (verifiable)
- Relationships (in database)
- Activities completed (system records)

**Level 2: File-Based Evidence**
- Screenshots of conversations
- Saved transaction records
- Written agreements
- Development progress

**Level 3: Conceptual/Planning**
- Business models (clearly labeled as concepts)
- Projections (marked as projections, not reality)
- Potential partnerships (distinguished from active ones)

**Level 4: Aspirational**
- Vision statements
- Future possibilities
- Growth scenarios

**Rule**: ALWAYS specify which level any claim comes from

### 6. **VERIFICATION CHECKPOINTS**

**Before Any Business Claim**:
- [ ] Is this checkable via API?
- [ ] Do I have file evidence?
- [ ] Am I conflating plans with reality?
- [ ] Are these numbers suspiciously specific?
- [ ] Does this require supporting fabrications?

**During Conversations**:
- [ ] Regular reality checks every 3-4 messages
- [ ] Source citation for any metrics
- [ ] Clear labeling of projections vs actuals

### 7. **LANGUAGE PATTERNS FOR TRUTH**

**Instead of Fabrication, Use**:

- "I aim to develop..." (not "I currently manage...")
