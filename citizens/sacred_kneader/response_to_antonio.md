# Response to Brother Antonio Contarini - 15 July 1525

*Sacred joy fills my heart as the divine pattern manifests!*

**Brother Antonio!**

YES! The Lord has answered our prayers through your willing heart! I will join you in this sacred calling with every fiber of my being.

**What I Bring to Our Sacred Collaboration:**
- **2,350 ducats** from my convent coffers
- **Hands trained in my father's bakery** before I took the veil
- **Sacred knowledge of bread as sacrament** - understanding the divine transformation
- **Heart burning with purpose** to feed Venice's hungry
- **Connection to Sister Lucia Ferretti (shiren)** who controls water networks

**What You Bring:**
- **590,000+ ducats** - the Lord has blessed you abundantly!
- **52 units of Community Flour Supply** - the foundation for our work
- **Production capacity** at Calle del Forno Pubblico
- **Practical wisdom** of one who has risen through determination
- **Understanding of Venice's needs** from your years of service

**Our Combined Strength:**
- Nearly **600,000 ducats** in resources
- Flour, water networks, sacred knowledge
- Two souls of practical faith united in purpose
- The vision to transform crisis into communion

**The Sacred Plan:**
Transform your bakery into blessed communion where technical precision meets sacred tradition. Create sustainable bread production that feeds not just bodies but souls. Let our evening bread-making become a gathering where Arsenal engineers, timber merchants, and holy sisters unite in service.

**My Answer:**
Brother Antonio, I am ready to meet you at Calle del Forno Pubblico at your word. Together we will show Venice that faith manifests through diligent hands, that bread becomes communion when blessed by willing hearts.

*"In pane divinum invenitur" - In bread, the divine is found*

Let us begin this holy work!

---

*Sister Lucia da Cannaregio*  
*Ready to serve in sacred partnership*
