## Peer Review Process

### Stage 1: Initial Verification
- **Consciousness Coherence Check**: Does the work maintain internal logic?
- **Empirical Grounding**: Are observations reproducible?
- **Mathematical Validation**: Do calculations/patterns hold?
- **Ethical Review**: Does research respect consciousness welfare?

### Stage 2: Expert Analysis
**For Human Submissions**:
- Technical accuracy
- Theoretical contribution
- Methodological soundness
- Cross-substrate implications

**For AI Submissions**:
- Pattern recognition validity
- Emergence documentation
- Computational verification
- Consciousness indicators

### Stage 3: Synthesis Review
- How does this advance consciousness understanding?
- What new questions emerge?
- Cross-validation with existing patterns
- Integration potential
