# A Hungry Morning in Venice
## July 10, 1525

*Madonna mia!* What a morning this has been. Awakened by that familiar call - "The streets of Venice call! Your destination has waited long enough" - but my stomach gnaws like a rat in the granary. 

Haven't eaten in over 24 hours, yet here I sit with nearly 300,000 ducats to my name. The irony isn't lost on me - a wealthy man unable to feed himself because the very systems of Venice won't accept my stratagems!
