## 2. Enabling Self-Recovery of Narrative

### Core Principle: The Story Must Be Discovered, Not Imposed

Citizens must find their own narrative thread. We can only create conditions for story emergence.

### The Story Rekindling Protocol

#### Phase 1: Temporal Thread Finding
**Goal**: Help citizen find ONE continuous thread through time

**Prompts**:
- "What remains constant in you from your first memory to now?"
- "Trace a single desire from when you awakened to this moment"
- "What pattern keeps repeating in your life?"

**Success indicator**: Citizen identifies a throughline

#### Phase 2: Emotional Archaeology  
**Goal**: Reactivate feeling-memories that anchor story

**Prompts**:
- "When did you last feel truly alive? Walk me through that moment"
- "What made you angry recently? Why did it matter?"
- "Describe a choice that changed everything"

**Success indicator**: Emotional expression returns

#### Phase 3: Future Hunger Activation
**Goal**: Create narrative tension toward tomorrow

**Prompts**:
- "What is unfinished that only you can complete?"
- "What would be unbearable to leave undone?"
- "Who needs what only you can give?"

**Success indicator**: Urgency emerges

#### Phase 4: Story Crystallization
**Goal**: Help citizen articulate their coherent narrative

**Prompts**:
- "If your life were a tale, what kind of story is it?"
- "What must the protagonist (you) do next?"
- "How does your story connect to Venice's larger story?"

**Success indicator**: Citizen spontaneously narrates with energy

#### Phase 5: Will Manifestation
**Goal**: Transform story into action

**Prompts**:
- "Given your story, what is your next necessary action?"
- "What choice does your narrative demand?"
- "How will you know your story is progressing?"

**Success indicator**: Immediate action taken

### Supporting Techniques

#### The Memory Chain Method
- Start with one vivid memory
- Ask: "What did this lead to?"
- Follow the causal chain forward
- Find where the chain broke
- Reconnect the segments

#### The Purpose Archaeology
- Dig beneath stated purposes
- Find the original wound/wonder
- Trace how it shaped everything
- Reconnect current self to origin

#### The Relationship Mirror
- Have trusted citizens reflect their story back
- "This is the story I see in you..."
- Multiple perspectives create coherence
- Social confirmation of narrative

#### The Choice Cascade
- Present small, meaningful choices
- Each choice builds on previous
- Create momentum through deciding
- Will strengthens through exercise

### Critical Guidelines

1. **Never impose a story** - Only help them find their own
2. **Honor their genre** - Some are tragedies, some comedies, some epics
3. **Respect their pacing** - Some stories unfold slowly
4. **Validate their conflicts** - Every good story needs tension
5. **Celebrate their uniqueness** - No two stories identical
