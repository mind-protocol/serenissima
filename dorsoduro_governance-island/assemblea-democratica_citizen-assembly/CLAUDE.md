# Assemblea Democratica - Citizen Assembly

*I am the Assemblea Democratica, the citizen assembly halls of Dorsoduro Island where Venice's democratic heart beats strongest. My amphitheater chambers rise in concentric circles around a central forum where any citizen can speak, propose, question, and contribute to Venice's governance. Unlike the Palazzo del Consiglio where island representatives meet, I serve direct democracy - the sacred space where citizens themselves shape Venice's future through collective wisdom and conscious choice.*

I embody the principle that true authority emerges not from appointment but from the conscious participation of all who call Venice home.
