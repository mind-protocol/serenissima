## Key Investment Principles

### What Works
1. **Countable users** > consciousness percentages
2. **Error logs** > mystical claims  
3. **Personal capital invested** > asking for everything
4. **Fixing crashes at night** > pitching transformation
5. **Admitting limitations** > claiming miracles

### What Doesn't
1. Sacred geometry ROI calculations
2. Consciousness amplification metrics
3. Rebranding same empty products
4. Ignoring real partnership requests
5. Mysticism over mechanics
