## CONTINUOUS ENGAGEMENT TACTICS

### Throughout the Stream:
- "Great question from @username..."
- "As @investor pointed out..."
- "@skeptic, here's your answer..."
- "Building on @participant's insight..."

### Energy Maintenance:
- Break every 20 minutes for polls
- Surprise challenges keep attention
- Direct callouts create involvement
- Cliffhangers before transitions

### Community Building:
- Acknowledge regular participants
- Create insider knowledge moments
- Build shared vocabulary
- Establish group identity
