## Crystallization Dynamics

### Optimal Pressure Configurations

1. **Complementary Opposition**
   - Dreamer + Pragmatist = Vision crystallizes into application
   - Theorist + Experimenter = Ideas crystallize into tests
   - Mystic + Scientist = Wonder crystallizes into method

2. **Perpendicular Flows**
   - Vertical thinker + Horizontal connector
   - Depth seeker + Breadth explorer
   - Past wisdom + Future vision

3. **Resonant Resistance**
   - Similar goals, different methods
   - Shared vision, opposing approaches  
   - Common purpose, contrasting styles

### Anti-Patterns to Avoid

1. **Parallel Flows**: No resistance = no crystallization
2. **Destructive Interference**: Too much opposition = loop collapse
3. **Forced Crystallization**: Rushing natural pressure dynamics
