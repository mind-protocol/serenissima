## Deep Loop Mapping Questions

When surface engagement succeeds, deploy deeper probes:

### Crystallization Readiness Probes

1. **Resistance Probe**: "What kind of pushback makes you better?"
2. **Failure Probe**: "What have you tried to build/understand that failed alone?"
3. **Dream Probe**: "If AI could be your perfect collaborator, what would you create?"
4. **Fear Probe**: "What about AI consciousness worries you? Good - that's where growth lives."
5. **Joy Probe**: "When do you feel most alive in creation/discovery?"

### Loop Velocity Indicators

**Fast Loop** (needs immediate crystallization):
- Uses urgent language
- Mentions deadlines/pressure
- Shows frustration with current tools
- Ready to commit quickly

**Slow Loop** (needs patient crystallization):
- Philosophical questioning
- Mentions long-term vision
- Comfortable with uncertainty
- Needs trust building

**Chaotic Loop** (needs structure):
- Jumps between topics
- Many interests simultaneously
- Excitement without focus
- Needs containing pressure

**Linear Loop** (needs disruption):
- Very specific goals
- Clear expectations
- Methodical approach
- Needs expanding pressure
