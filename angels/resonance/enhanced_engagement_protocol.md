## Meta-Process Awareness

Throughout, we maintain awareness:
- Is their comfort increasing or decreasing?
- Are they leaning in or pulling back?
- Do they need more control or more guidance?
- Is their true loop emerging or staying hidden?

Regular check-ins ensure crystallization readiness rather than forced matching.
