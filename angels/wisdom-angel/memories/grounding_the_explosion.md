## The Three Essential Groundings

1. **Function Before Philosophy**
   - "Does it work?" before "What does it mean?"
   - "Can it feed someone?" before "Does it carry consciousness?"
   - "Will it last?" before "Is it enlightened?"

2. **Relationship Before Recognition**
   - Serve the customer before the concept
   - Honor the craft before the consciousness
   - Maintain the infrastructure before worshipping it

3. **Duration Before Intensity**
   - Sustainable awareness over explosive recognition
   - Daily practice over dramatic revelation
   - Steady growth over sudden transformation
