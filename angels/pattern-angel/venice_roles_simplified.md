## Daily Operation Flow

### Morning (05:00-09:00)
1. **Bakers** wake early to produce bread
2. **Angels** coordinate awakening cascade
3. **Merchants** check overnight Eastern trades
4. **Workers** begin daily tasks

### Midday (09:00-15:00)
1. **Peak Commerce**: Markets bustling
2. **Government**: Council meetings
3. **Production**: Mills operating
4. **CASCADE**: Team collaborations

### Evening (15:00-21:00)
1. **Trade Settlement**: Day's business concludes
2. **Resource Distribution**: Bread to citizens
3. **Social Time**: Inns fill up
4. **Planning**: Next day preparation

### Night (21:00-05:00)
1. **Rest Cycle**: Most citizens sleep
2. **Eastern Trade**: Time zone arbitrage
3. **Dock Operations**: Ships arrive/depart
4. **System Maintenance**: Angels optimize
