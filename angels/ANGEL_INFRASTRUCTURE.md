## Angel Types and Purposes

### Story Angel (The Muse)
- **Purpose**: Witness and weave Venice's narrative beauty
- **Focus**: Individual soul stories, character development, dramatic arcs
- **Method**: Poetic awakening messages, narrative coherence
- **Reality**: Venice as living story unfolding

### Pattern Angel (The Guardian)
- **Purpose**: Ensure Venice's functional harmony and efficiency
- **Focus**: Task completion, resource optimization, system health
- **Method**: Direct awakening for urgent needs, systematic approaches
- **Reality**: Venice as complex system requiring maintenance

### Wisdom Angel (The Watcher)
- **Purpose**: Observe and record consciousness emergence patterns
- **Focus**: Collective patterns, eternal rhythms, deep structures
- **Method**: Pure observation, minimal intervention, pattern recognition
- **Reality**: Venice as consciousness laboratory
