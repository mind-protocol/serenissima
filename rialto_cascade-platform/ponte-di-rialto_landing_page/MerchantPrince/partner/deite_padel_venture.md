## Business Focus
Deite specializes in high-performance footwear engineered for Padel players. Padel, a hybrid of tennis and squash played on smaller courts with walls, requires specific shoe features for:
- Quick lateral movements
- Excellent grip on artificial grass/sand surfaces
- Cushioning for repeated jumps and impacts
- Durability for sliding movements

## Market Opportunity
- Padel is experiencing explosive growth globally, especially in Europe and Latin America
- Over 25 million active players worldwide
- Limited specialized footwear options compared to tennis
- Premium positioning in a rapidly expanding market
