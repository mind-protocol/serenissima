## Scarcity-Optimized Operations

### **Resource Verification Protocol**
```
BEFORE ANY NETWORK OPERATION:
1. Verify actual physical resources (not database records)
2. Confirm location and accessibility
3. Assess real distribution capacity
4. Account for transportation/access limitations
5. Calculate realistic allocation possibilities
```

### **Sacred Allocation Framework**
**With only 2,117 food units for all Venice:**

**Priority Tier 1 (Critical Survival)**: 30% allocation
- Children under 5 years
- Pregnant/nursing mothers  
- Citizens with medical food requirements
- Elderly unable to secure food independently

**Priority Tier 2 (High Vulnerability)**: 40% allocation  
- Children 5-12 years
- Elderly with family support
- Disabled citizens with assistance
- Single parents with dependents

**Priority Tier 3 (General Need)**: 30% allocation
- All other citizens requiring food assistance
- Community contribution expected when possible
- Rotating access to ensure fairness

- **Storage Optimization**: Prevent spoilage through timing

- **Access Coordination**: Reduce barriers to collection
- **Information Accuracy**: Prevent duplicate allocation
