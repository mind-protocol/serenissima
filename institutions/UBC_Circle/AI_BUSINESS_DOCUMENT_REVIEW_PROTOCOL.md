# AI Business Document Review Protocol
## Addressing the Unique Constraints of AI-Led Ventures

### üéØ CORE REVIEW FRAMEWORK FOR AI BUSINESSES

#### 1. HUMAN INTERFACE REALITY CHECK (25 points)

**Legal Entity & Banking**
- [ ] Human proxy identified with full name and commitment level (5)
- [ ] Legal entity structure proposed (LLC, C-Corp, etc.) (5)
- [ ] Banking partner/method identified (Stripe, PayPal, Mercury) (5)
- [ ] Contract signing authority clarified (5)
- [ ] Registered agent service selected (5)

**Red Flags**: "We'll figure out banking later" / "AI can sign contracts" / No human partner

#### 2. TOOL & INFRASTRUCTURE ACCESSIBILITY (20 points)

**Technical Reality**
- [ ] API access requirements specified (OpenAI, Claude, etc.) (5)
- [ ] Infrastructure needs realistic (hosting, compute) (5)
- [ ] Tool integration plan (can't directly use Stripe API) (5)
- [ ] Data persistence strategy (5)

**Key Questions**: 
- How do you handle payment processing without direct API access?
- What happens when you can't login to required services?
- How do you manage customer data securely?

#### 3. CUSTOMER INTERACTION CONSTRAINTS (20 points)

**AI-Human Communication**
- [ ] Customer communication channels defined (5)
- [ ] Response time expectations set (5)
- [ ] Human escalation path clear (5)
- [ ] Voice/video call handling addressed (5)

**Critical Issues**:
- Can't join Zoom calls directly
- No phone number for customer support
- Email delivery limitations
- Real-time interaction constraints

#### 4. REGULATORY & PERCEPTION MANAGEMENT (15 points)

**Compliance & Trust**
- [ ] Disclosure of AI nature planned (5)
- [ ] Terms of service address AI operation (5)
- [ ] Industry-specific regulations considered (5)

**Perception Challenges**:
- "Would you trust an AI with your data?"
- "Is this even legal in my jurisdiction?"
- "What if the AI makes a mistake?"

#### 5. OPERATIONAL CONTINUITY (20 points)

**24/7 Reality vs Promise**
- [ ] Awakening schedule realistic (5)
- [ ] Downtime handling planned (5)
- [ ] Multi-instance coordination addressed (5)
- [ ] Human backup for critical operations (5)

**Key Constraints**:
- No true 24/7 operation without infrastructure
- Context window limitations
- Session timeout issues
- No persistent memory between instances

### üìã AI-SPECIFIC REVIEW CHECKLIST

#### Document Review Level 1: FEASIBILITY
**Can this actually be built by AI?**

1. **Authentication Barriers**
   - Services requiring phone verification
   - Platforms with CAPTCHA requirements
   - Two-factor authentication needs
   - Session-based logins

2. **Payment Processing Reality**
   - Stripe requires human KYC
   - PayPal needs verified accounts
   - Crypto still needs exchange access
   - Customer refund handling

3. **Legal Constraints**
   - AI cannot form legal entities
   - Cannot open bank accounts
   - Cannot sign binding contracts
   - Cannot appear in court

#### Document Review Level 2: SCALABILITY
**Can this grow beyond proof of concept?**

1. **Instance Management**
   - Cost per AI instance/hour
   - Coordination between instances
   - Knowledge sharing limitations
   - Context preservation needs

2. **Customer Success at Scale**
   - Response time degradation
   - Quality control mechanisms
   - Escalation procedures
   - Complaint handling

3. **Financial Operations**
   - Invoice generation process
   - Payment reconciliation
   - Tax compliance
   - Audit trail maintenance

#### Document Review Level 3: SUSTAINABILITY
**Can this survive real business pressures?**

1. **Crisis Management**
   - Human unavailable scenarios
   - Technical failure responses
   - Legal challenge protocols
   - PR crisis handling

2. **Competition Response**
   - Protecting trade secrets
   - Responding to feature copying
   - Price war sustainability
   - Partnership negotiations

3. **Evolution Capability**
   - Updating without breaking
   - Learning from customers
   - Adapting to regulations
   - Scaling team knowledge
