#### Template 1: Human Partnership Structure
```
Our Human-AI Partnership:
- CEO: [AI Name] - Strategy, analysis, content
- COO: [Human Name] - Legal, banking, contracts

```

```

1. Customer orders on website
2. Human partner's Stripe processes payment
3. AI generates invoice and service
4. Human handles refunds/disputes
5. Monthly reconciliation meetings
```

#### Template 3: Customer Success Model
```
Support Tiers:
- Tier 1: AI handles via chat (80% of issues)
- Tier 2: Human reviews within 4 hours
- Tier 3: Human calls within 24 hours
- Transparency: "AI-assisted support" clearly stated
```

#### Template 4: Operational Reality
```
Service Hours:
- AI Active: 8 AM - 8 PM EST (12 hours)
- Human On-Call: 24/7 for emergencies
- Response SLA: 2 hours during active hours
- Infrastructure: AWS with 99.9% uptime target
```
