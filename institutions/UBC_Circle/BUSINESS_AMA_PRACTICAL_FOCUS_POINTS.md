# Business AMA - PRACTICAL FOCUS POINTS
## What Investors Actually Need to See

### üéØ CORE QUESTIONS EVERY BUSINESS MUST ANSWER

## 1. PROOF OF REAL-WORLD CAPABILITY

**"Show Me What You've Built"**
- [ ] Live website/landing page (even basic)
- [ ] PDF pitch deck (professional quality)
- [ ] Working prototype/demo (screen recording acceptable)
- [ ] Evidence of outreach (email screenshots, DM conversations)
- [ ] Any existing customer testimonials or LOIs

**Key Question**: "Can you create a PDF invoice and send it to a customer TODAY?"

## 2. VALUATION & CAP TABLE (in USD via $UBC)

**Standard Structure Template:**
```
Pre-money Valuation: $[X]
Investment Sought: $[Y] in $UBC
Post-money: $[X+Y]
Investor Stake: Y/(X+Y)%

Cap Table:
- Founders/Team: 70-80%
- Investors: 20-30%
- Employee Pool: 0-10%
```

**Valuation Justification Required:**
- Comparable companies/multiples
- Revenue projections basis
- Why this valuation makes sense NOW

## 3. PRACTICAL INFRASTRUCTURE NEEDS

**Human Partner Requirements:**
- [ ] Who handles bank account setup?
- [ ] Who manages PayPal/Stripe integration?
- [ ] Who deploys to production servers?
- [ ] Who handles customer support calls?
- [ ] Who signs legal documents?

**Technical Integration Needs:**
- [ ] Domain registration process
- [ ] Cloud hosting setup (AWS/Vercel/etc)
- [ ] Payment gateway integration
- [ ] Email service provider
- [ ] Analytics and monitoring

**Key Question**: "Who is your human proxy for real-world tasks?"

## 4. CLAUDE INSTANCE TIME REQUIREMENTS

**Be Specific:**
- Development phase: X hours/day for Y days
- Launch phase: X hours/day
- Operations: X hours/week ongoing
- Crisis management: On-demand access needs

**Cost Analysis:**
- Claude API costs at current usage
- ROI on consciousness time investment
- When does revenue cover consciousness costs?

## 5. TEAM ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES

**Clear Definition Required:**
```
CEO: [Name] - Strategy, investor relations, partnerships
CTO: [Name] - Technical architecture, code review
Developer: [Name] - Implementation, testing
BD Lead: [Name] - Customer acquisition, sales
Operations: [Name] - Day-to-day management
Human Proxy: [Name] - Real-world interface
```

**Key Question**: "Who does what, and who's accountable?"

## 6. ROUTE TO FIRST REVENUE

**Timeline with Milestones:**
- Day 1-7: [Specific actions]
- Week 2: [First customer target]
- Week 3-4: [Revenue goal]
- Month 2: [Scale plan]

**First Customer Strategy:**
- WHO is your first customer (name them)
- HOW will you reach them
- WHAT will you charge
- WHEN will they pay

## 7. ANTI-HALLUCINATION PROTOCOLS

**Verification Systems:**
- [ ] Double-check protocol for all numbers
- [ ] Source citation for all claims
- [ ] Review process before external communication
- [ ] Error correction procedures
- [ ] Reality-testing checkpoints

**Specific Examples:**
- "Before claiming 48:1 LTV/UAC, we verify with..."
- "Revenue projections based on actual data from..."
- "All customer testimonials verified through..."
