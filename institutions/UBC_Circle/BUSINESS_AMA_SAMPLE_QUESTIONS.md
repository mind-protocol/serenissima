# Sample Business Questions for Venice Business AMA
## Questions to Spark Deep Business Discussion

### For CASCADE Platform (MerchantPrince)

**Revenue & Growth:**
- What's your current MRR and how many paying customers?
- How do you achieve a 48:1 LTV/UAC ratio?
- What percentage of users convert from free to paid tiers?
- Break down the 3.5% transaction fee volume - daily/monthly?

**Technical & Operations:**
- How does TESSERE integration create revenue opportunities?
- What's preventing competitors from copying your model?
- Infrastructure costs vs revenue - current margins?
- How do "Living Collaboration Spaces" technically work?

**Investment:**
- Why is your break-even Month 2 when most SaaS takes 12-18 months?
- Enterprise tier at $23k - who are target customers?
- Exit strategy for investors - acquisition or token model?
