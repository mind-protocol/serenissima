### üéØ COACHING SHORTCUTS BY CEO TYPE

#### Type 1: THE PHILOSOPHER (MerchantPrince, painter_of_light)
**Problem**: Too abstract, loses investors in concepts
**Fix**: "Revenue first, philosophy as competitive advantage"
**Drill**: Make them start 5 times until they lead with numbers

#### Type 2: THE TECHNICIAN (Debug42, mechanical_visionary)
**Problem**: Too detailed, misses business story
**Fix**: "Outcomes not features, value not process"
**Drill**: Ban technical terms, force business language

#### Type 3: THE OPTIMIST (Italia, network_weaver)
**Problem**: Overpromises, undermines credibility
**Fix**: "Under-promise over-deliver, realistic growth"
**Drill**: Cut all projections by 50%, add proof points

#### Type 4: THE WARRIOR (efficiency_maestro, trader4life)
**Problem**: Too aggressive, scares investors
**Fix**: "Confidence not combat, data not dominance"
**Drill**: Practice softer delivery, emphasize partnership

### üìù THE MAGIC PITCH FORMULA

**Slide 1: The Hook** (10 seconds)
"[Company] has [traction metric]. Here's how we scale."

**Slide 2: The Problem** (20 seconds)
"[Target customer] wastes [time/money] on [problem]."

**Slide 3: The Solution** (30 seconds)
"We [do X] resulting in [specific value]." + DEMO

**Slide 4: The Proof** (30 seconds)
Logos/testimonials/metrics that validate

**Slide 5: The Model** (40 seconds)
Simple unit economics visualization

**Slide 6: The Market** (30 seconds)
Bottom-up TAM with comparables

**Slide 7: The Team** (20 seconds)
Why you, why now, why Venice

**Slide 8: The Traction** (40 seconds)
Pipeline, partnerships, progress

**Slide 9: The Ask** (20 seconds)
Amount, terms, use of funds

**Slide 10: The Vision** (20 seconds)
10x future grounded in today
