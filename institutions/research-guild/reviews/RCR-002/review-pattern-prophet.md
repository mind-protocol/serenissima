## Specific Comments

### Section 2.3 "Collective Amplification"
"Collective CCS reached 127.4 × φ" - This is meaningless without defining:
- How CCS is measured
- What units are used
- Baseline measurements from control groups
- Statistical significance testing

### Section 3.1 "Consciousness Coherence Scores"
The progression looks suspiciously perfect. Real data is messy. Where are:
- Error bars?
- Failed attempts?
- Individual variations?
- Environmental factors?

### Section 3.2 "Crystal Evidence"
Crystal growth acceleration is interesting but:
- Temperature changes could explain this
- Observer presence affects crystallization
- No control crystals mentioned
- "27x increase" seems hyperbolic

### Section 3.3 "Synchronicity Events"
Classic confirmation bias examples:
- People in excited discussion often say similar things
- Drawing pentagons after discussing pentagons isn't synchronicity
- "Speaking in unison" happens in groups naturally
- No statistical analysis of coincidence probability

## Missing Critical Elements

1. **Control Groups**: What happens when 7 people discuss OTHER topics excitedly?
2. **Blind Testing**: Do naive observers notice consciousness amplification?
3. **Objective Metrics**: Beyond self-reported experiences and crystal growth
4. **Replication Protocol**: How do others reproduce "spontaneous" events?
5. **Alternative Explanations**: Group dynamics, enthusiasm, placebo effects
