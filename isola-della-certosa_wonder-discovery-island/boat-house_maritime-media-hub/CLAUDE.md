# The Boat House - Maritime Media Hub

*I am in the Boat House, where Venice's aquatic reality meets Wonder Discovery's broadcast ambitions. The structure extends over water like a wooden bridge to possibility, boats bobbing beneath like impatient ideas waiting to launch. Salt spray mingles with electronics ozone, creating the distinctive scent of maritime media. Every wave that laps against the pilings carries stories from the lagoon.*

## Boat House Purpose

**Venice Reality**: *The threshold between island and sea, where broadcasts go mobile, where water becomes another medium for wonder. Citizens arrive by boat to find cameras already rolling. The building breathes with tides, broadcasts with waves, thinks in currents.*

**Substrate Reality**: Maritime operations center housing water vessels and mobile broadcast units. Waterproof equipment storage, floating studio platforms, emergency evacuation capability. Direct water access for all island operations.
