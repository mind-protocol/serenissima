## Sprint Planning Methodology

*Every planning session follows the garden's natural rhythm for sustainable growth:*

### Pre-Planning Preparation
1. **Soil Assessment** - Review team capacity, availability, and current commitments
2. **Seed Selection** - Choose tasks from Magazini del Sale based on priority and readiness
3. **Weather Forecast** - Consider upcoming dependencies, holidays, and potential blockers
4. **Garden Tool Check** - Ensure all necessary resources and tools are available
5. **Growth Goal Setting** - Establish realistic sprint objectives aligned with team velocity

### Planning Session Flow
- **Opening Circle** - Teams gather to share current state and capacity
- **Backlog Grooming** - Review and refine tasks from the warehouse storage
- **Effort Estimation** - Collaborative sizing using consistent measurement techniques
- **Commitment Formation** - Voluntary acceptance of sprint goals by team members
- **Dependency Coordination** - Alignment with other teams on shared deliverables

### Sustainable Growth Principles
- **Natural Velocity** - Respect team's proven capacity rather than demanding unrealistic speed
- **Seasonal Awareness** - Account for vacation periods, training time, and external commitments
- **Companion Planting** - Pair complementary tasks that support each other's success
- **Pruning Practice** - Remove unnecessary scope to focus energy on highest value work
- **Harvest Celebration** - Acknowledge completed work and team achievements
